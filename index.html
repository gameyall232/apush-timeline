United States announces plans to launch satellites within 3 years

July 29, 1955

President Eisenhowever endorses the International Geophysical Year proposed launch of small earth-orbiting satellites, the first of their kind.

Soviet Union approves project to launch a satellite using a missile

August 30, 1955

The Soviet Academy of Sciences approved the project and work on it began, the satellite being named ‘Object-D’.
